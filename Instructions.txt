pyABA Math in GNU/Linux system

Requirement:
-----------
python-qt4,espeak


USAGE:
------
First Select two Operands as value1 and value 2
Select the operator. 
Confirm Level
Click 'show' button on top right

Level 1
	Listen to the speech and then say & click on the values

Level 2
	Listen to the speech and then say & click the correct equation

Level 3	
	Say and Click the answer to the operation displayed and heard

Level 4
	Say and Click on the correct answer to the operation shown

NOTE:
-----
In all the Levels, after a certain time the correct answer, will blink and speech will be heard again to assist the child to get the answer.
